How to use this tool:
BEFORE YOUR START WITH A NEW ORGANIZATION HIT NEW ORG

When you DO NOT have a Customer Project in SFDC:
  • Step 1. Login/navigate to your Coveo Cloud Organization
  • Step 2. Click on Organization
  • Step 3. Navigate to your Public search page.
  • Step 4. Execute a search in the standalone searchbox.
  • Step 5. When results are displayed. Start typing a query. Hit submit.
  • Step 6. Open or Quickview one of the results.
  • Step 7. Go back to your search page.
  • Step 8. Now, activate Search Tracker.
  • Step 9. Execute a single search.
  • The search query will also be checked when you enable the Search Tracker
  • Step 10. Click on Implementation

When you have a Customer Project in SFDC:
  • The Sfdc information will be kept after RESET!
  • Step 1. Navigate to the Customer Project in SFDC.
  • Step 2. Click on Sfdc and Get Sfdc Info. Or manually enter the below info.
  • Step 3. Login/navigate to your Coveo Cloud Organization
  • Step 4. Click on Organization
  • Step 5. Click on Sfdc.
  • Step 6. Click on Open search page. A new tab will be opened.
  • Step 7 Execute a search in the standalone searchbox.
  • Step 8. When results are displayed. Start typing a query. Hit submit.
  • Step 9. Open or Quickview one of the results.
  • Step 10. Go back to your search page.
  • Step 11. Now, activate Search Tracker.
  • Step 12. Execute a single search.
  • The search query will also be checked when you enable the Search Tracker
  • Step 13. Click on Implementation
  • Step 14. If you want to push it to the Coveo Index: Push
